Steps to Make your Bed

In 3 minutes or less.


        "If you can do the small task correct you wont be able to do the big tasks correct."
      

Good Morning bed. What a mess.

        "Admiral McRaven succinctly emphasizes the transformative power of personal change in shaping the world.

        to begin with modest steps and seek solace in each endeavor.

        and effecting substantial change in the wider world."
      


Step One

            Pillow punch!
            If you have 100% down pillows, you can easily restore them to perfection with a simple technique. 
            Begin by giving each pillow a quick shake to loosen the down filling. 
            Then, deliver a double punch to the sides of the pillows, ensuring a firm yet gentle impact. 
            This action helps to redistribute the feathers and fluff up the pillows, 
            bringing them back to their original plumpness
        


Step Two

            Pillow pat!
            Lightly pat the center of each pillow a couple of times after giving them a gentle punch. 
            This brisk motion helps to redistribute the filling and restore their plumpness, 
            resulting in a desirable fluffiness that adds to the overall perfection of the pillows.
        


Step Three

            Place pillows!
            At the top of the bed like so.
        


Step Four

            "Begin by locating and pulling one corner of the comforter's duvet cover. 
            Bring it towards the corresponding corner of the mattress. 
            Repeat this process for each of the four corners, 
            ensuring that the comforter is right side up and evenly spread across the bed."" 
        


Step Five

            "You have now successfully spread out the comforter, ensuring it is properly arranged. 
            The only remaining steps are to fold down the access comforter and straighten the duvet cover, 
            resulting in a clean and crisp finished look."
        


Step Six

            "Fold down the top of the bed by pillows, 
            giving the underside of fold a quick upward chop to straighten the comforter and duvet cover.

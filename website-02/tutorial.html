Steps to Make your Bed

In 3 minutes or less.


        "If you can do the small task correct you wont be able to do the big tasks correct."
      

Good Morning bed. What a mess.

        What Admiral McRaven is saying simple put.
        If you want to change the world, change your own world first. 
        Start small with a simply task that provides a feeling of acomplitment. 
        Then if your day goes bad you have a nice made bed to come home to.


Step One

            Pillow punch!
            I have 100% down pillows a quick shake and a double punch to the sides of the pillows 
            fluffs them back to perfection.


Step Two

            Pillow pat!
            A lite brisk couple of pats to the center of the pillow, 
            after punching,
            fluffs pillows out to perfection.
        


Step Three

            Place pillows at the top of the bed like so.
        


Step Four

            Locate and pull a corner of the comfortors duvet cover corner,
            pulling it to an individual corner of the matress's corner. 
            Repeat for each four corners.
            Making sure comfort is right side up and placed evenly over the bed.
        


Step Five

            You now have the comfortor properly spread out like so.
            Only needing to fold down access comforter and straightening duvet cover for a clean crisp vinised look.
        


Step Six

            Fold down eccess comforter at the top of the bed by pillows, giving the underside of the fold 
            at fold point, a quick upward chop to straighten devet and devet cover.
            Wallaa! You now have a quick accomplishment to be proud of and a neat and orderly bed to come home to. 
            I dont know about you but this is a huge deal to me, and my OCD.

Pumpkin Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tespoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup Crisco Lard
  • 1 cup LIBBY'S 100% Pure Pumkin
  • 1 large eggs

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Mix Flour, baking soda, cimmamon, and salt. Set asside
  3. Cut Crisco Lard into pumkin, egg, sugar and mix.
  4. (Do not over mix, there should be small lumps of lard remaining in mix.)
  5. Combine dry ingredents with wet ingredents.
  6. (This is when you will blend the rest of the lard lumps out.)
  7. Drop golf ball size on cookie mix on cookie sheetabout 1/2 inch apart
  8. Bake 12 to 13 minutes
  9. Let cool and ice with frosting of your choice.
  10. Cream Cheese frosting is highly recommended

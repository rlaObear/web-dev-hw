Pumpkin Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tespoon backing soda
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 cup crisco
  • 1 cup LIBBY'S 100% Pure Pumkin
  • 2 large eggs

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Combine and set aside
  3. Cut Crisco into pumkin and sugar
  4. Combine dry ingredents
  5. Drop on cookie sheet
  6. Bake 13 minutes
  7. Let cool and ice with Cream Cheese Frosting
